I started college through Running Start at Eastern Washington University. Like many freshman, I was unsure of what I wanted to do, but I looked forward to the possibilites. I took many of the core classes to try out several different fields before I declared a major in Communication Studies. After a couple quarters of Communications classes, I realized that while I really enjoyed the classes, I didn't really want the jobs that field would lead to. I had been considering a minor in Visual Communication Design, but I decided to make it my major. I love my classes, have a 3.8 cumulative GPA, and will be graduating in December 2014.

Background information

I was also involved in several extra curicular activites including swim team, synchronized swimming, band, and Spokane Youth Symphony. I also have a passion for words and books, which led me to a job with Spokane County Library District. I've worked there for over 8 years now working my way up through the positions.
